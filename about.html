How To:

  • Enter your favorite, author, subject, or book title (or mix and match any of those three).
  • Click find match and like or dislike the books shown (if you've read them great, if not try to judge based on the cover and description).
  • To see the description of a book click the info button in the bottom right corner.
  • After a certain number of likes and dislikes you will be matched with a book based on your preferences.

About:

  • The books are pulled from the Google Books API.
  • To accomodate for the union of inputs rather than taking an intersection, each input the user enters into the text boxes are performed as a query seperately.
  • Another more specific query of the intersection of all three inputs is performed for more precise results. All of these results are then aggregated.
  • In order to find matches, information about the books are hashed into specific hash maps and collisions are counted based on likes.
  • Taking these properties it performs a new query and matches the user with a random book from the new query list.
